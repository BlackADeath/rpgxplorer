Sample Campaign
Campaign Overview

Background

The campaign begins in....where the players are approached by a Dwarf trader with red hair and a penchant for fat cigars.

Campaign Objectives

The party must head North to investigate why the spring caravan from ..... has not arrived. The party will receive 100gp from the Trader's Guild for simply establishing the status of the caravan and a further 500gp a head if they discover any bandit involvement and deal with it, permanently. 

Lorimer, a middle-ranking bookkeeper for the guild will accompany the party to record what happens and to make decisions on behalf of the Guild, if they are required.
