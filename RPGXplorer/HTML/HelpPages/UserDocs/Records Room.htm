Records Room
Campaign Document

Located on the first floor landing, this room was once completely full of books, ledgers and parchments documenting the history and business of this community.

Everything is destroyed and the floor at the opposite end of the room has partially collapsed. An ornate desk of stone, metal and glass lies at a sixty degree angle within the hole created by the fire and the collapse. A blackened skeleton can just be made out partly crushed beneath the desk and wedged between it and the caved in floor.

The desk contains 3 drawers, each of which is locked (DC 25). The skeleton has a Ring of Protection +1 on its left hand but is well concealed by the effects of the fire (Search DC 25).
