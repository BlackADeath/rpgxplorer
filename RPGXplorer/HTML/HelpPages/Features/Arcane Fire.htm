Arcane Fire
Feature

Arcane Fire (Su): The ability to change arcane spell energy into arcane fire, manifesting it as a bolt of raw magical energy. The bolt is a ranged touch attack with long range (400 feet + 40 feet/level in granting class) that deals 1d6 points of damage per granting class level plus 1d6 points of damage per level of the spell used to create the effect. This ability costs one 9th-level spell slot.
