Gift of the Divine
Feature

Gift of the Divine (Su): Available only to characters with Cleric levels. Allows a character to transfer one or more uses of his turn undead ability to a willing creature. (Characters who rebuke undead transfer uses of rebuke undead instead.) The transfer lasts anywhere from 24 hours to one week (chosen at the time of transfer), and while the transfer is in effect, the number of turning attempts per day allowed to the character is reduced by the number transferred. The recipient turns undead as a cleric of the transferring character's cleric level but uses her own Charisma modifier.
