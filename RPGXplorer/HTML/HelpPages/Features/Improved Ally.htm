Improved Ally
Feature
Improved Ally: When the character casts a planar ally spell (including the lesser and greater versions), he makes a Diplomacy check to convince the creature to aid him for a reduced payment. If the character’s Diplomacy check adjusts the creature’s attitude to helpful the creature will work for 50% of the standard fee, as long as the task is one that is not against its nature.

The improved ally class feature only works when the planar ally shares at least one aspect of alignment with the character.

A character can have only one such ally at a time, but he may bargain for tasks from other planar allies normally.
