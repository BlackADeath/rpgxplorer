Smite Evil 3/Day
Feature

Smite Evil (Su): 3 times per day, the character may attempt to smite evil with one normal melee attack. She adds her Charisma bonus (if any) to her attack roll and deals 1 extra point of damage per level in the granting class. If the character accidentally smites a creature that is not evil, the smite has no effect, but the ability is still used up for that day.
