Summon Shadow
Feature

Summon Shadow (Su): The character can summon a shadow, an undead shade. Unlike a normal shadow, this shadow’s alignment matches that of the character, and the creature cannot create spawn.

The summoned shadow cannot be turned, rebuked, or commanded by any third party. This shadow serves as a companion to the character and can communicate intelligibly with him. 

If a shadow companion is destroyed, or the character chooses to dismiss it, the character must attempt a DC 15 Fortitude save. If the saving throw fails, the character loses 200 experience points per  level in the granting class. A successful saving throw reduces the loss by half. The character’s XP total can never go below 0 as the result of a shadow’s dismissal or destruction. A destroyed or dismissed shadow companion cannot be replaced for 30 days.

Improvements to this feature grant additional Hit Dice to the shadow as noted in the feature name (along with requisites base attack and save bonus increases).
